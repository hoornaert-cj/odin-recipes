How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Not recommended for novice chefs, a peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a difficult to make and doesn't taste all that good, but we are recommneding that you make one anyway. Involving bread of some sort, peanut butter of some sort, and jelly of some sort, this dish will fill your stomach and help you survive until your next (hopefully more delicious) meal.

  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Peanut Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Jelly (2 tablespoons)
  1. On one slice of bread, spread peanut butter evenly over the bread.
  2. On the other slice of bread, spread the jelly evenly over the bread.
  3. Put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing in.
  4. Serve and Enjoy (somewhat)!
